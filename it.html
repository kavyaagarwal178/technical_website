Information technology


what is information technology?


Information technology is a contempory term that describes the combination of computer technology(hardware,software) with telecommunications data.

usage

1- Education

1- email
2- distance learning

2- Health and medicine

1- Robots
2- Telemedicine

Functions

1- capture

This is a process of compiling information.

2- processing

The process of converting,analysing,computing and producing all forms of data is processing

3- generate

The process of organizing information into a useful form whether number,text,sound or visual image

4- store and retrieve

storage is a process of retaining information for future use
retrieval is a process by which a computer locates and copies stores data for future processing

5- transmit

The process of distributing information over a communication network for eg email
