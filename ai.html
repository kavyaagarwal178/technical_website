Artificial intelligence



What is artifical intelligence

1- Study of computer system that attempt to model and apply the intelligence of human mind.
2- A branch of computer science dealing with the stimulation of intelligent behaviour in computers.
3- The capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behaviour.


Principles


1- Reasoning,knowledge,planning,learning and communication
2- Perception and the ability to move and manipulate objects
3- It is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines,especially intelligent computer programs.

Advantages

1- More powerful and more useful computers.
2- New and improved interfaces.
3- Solving new problems.
4- Better handling of information.
5- Relieves information overload.

Disadvantages

1- Increased costs.
2- Difficulty with software development.
3- Few experienced programmers.
4- Few practical products have reached the mark yet.

Applications

Medicine

1- A medical clinic can use ai systems to organize bed schedules,make a staff rotation and provide medical information.
2- AI also has applications in field of cardiology,neurology,sonography and complex operations of internal organs.
3- It also has an application in image guided surgery and image analysis and enhancement.

Music

1- Scientists are trying to make the computer emulate the activities of the skillful musician.
2- Composition,performance,music theory,sound proceesing are the areas on which research in music and AI is going on.

telecommunications
